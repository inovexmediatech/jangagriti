Welcome

Shree Jana Jagriti Secondary School, a premier government institution, empowers students with quality education rooted in Nepali values. From our humble start in 2029 BS under a banyan tree, we've grown into a district leader, fostering academic rigor and character.

Our Journey

Established in summer 2029 Bikram Sambat as a small government school, we began with basic classes outdoors. Over time, dedication transformed us into a well-equipped hub known nationwide for realistic, impactful education blending tradition and progress.
